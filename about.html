Hi!

My name is Silja Mägi and I am a graphic designer based in Saaremaa. I have always liked creative activities, and in 2019 I started doing photography and graphic design.
In the beginning, photography was the main focus and graphic design received less attention. Now, however, things have changed, primarily because I went to further my graphic design education at Kuressaare Ametikool. I discovered that I am capable of much more than I could have imagined. In addition to developing skills, I have also learned new ones such as animating graphics and creating web pages using code. I started my studies at the beginning of 2021 and graduated in the summer of 2023.
