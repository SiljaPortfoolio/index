KVK logo ja CVI

New logo and CVI for Kuressaare Vanalinna School. The logo symbol combining the letters K and V has been inspired by the first letters of Kuressaare Vanalinna Kooli. The logo's splayed arms indicate openness and the rounded ends indicate friendliness. The logo as a whole conveys the current value of the school: innovative, involved, developing, learner-oriented.
Yellow color symbolizes joy, friendliness, positivity, interest and is also associated with learning ability.
Dark blue adds formality and symbolizes reliability, certainty.
The font of the logo is the sanserif Raleway, which highlights the modernity of the Kuressaare Vanalinna School.
A CVI with usage indicators has also been created.
